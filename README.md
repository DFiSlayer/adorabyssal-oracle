# 🌀 Adorabyssal Oracle

A digital companion for the **Adorabyssal Oracle** — a 36-card oracle deck featuring the cutest versions of mythological, supernatural, and cryptozoological creatures from around the world.

**🔗 Live App: [https://dfislayer.github.io/adorabyssal-oracle](https://dfislayer.github.io/adorabyssal-oracle)**

---

## Features

- **Single Draw** — tap the card back to draw a random card with a unique atmospheric reading for every one of the 36 creatures
- **3-Card Spread** — Past · Present · Future spread with animated slot machine reveal, individual card readings, and a Deep Synergy Analysis that cross-reads all three cards together
- **Browse** — scroll through all 36 cards with full lore, element, classification, and positivity
- **Optional Question** — type a question before drawing to receive a focused interpretation connecting your query to the card drawn
- **Installable PWA** — works like a native app on Android and iOS; add to your home screen for instant offline access

---

## The Deck

36 creatures drawn from global folklore, mythology, and cryptozoology — each with an associated element, classification, and theme:

| Element | Associations |
|---------|-------------|
| 🔥 Fire | Action, passion, desire, energy, destruction |
| 💨 Air | Thought, travel, intellect, communication, divination |
| 🌿 Earth | Stability, prosperity, home, nature, endurance |
| 💧 Water | Emotions, dreams, love, healing, rest |

**Classifications:** Cryptid · Mythological · Supernatural

**Positivity:** Positive · Neutral · Negative

## Installing on Android

1. Open **[https://dfislayer.github.io/adorabyssal-oracle](https://dfislayer.github.io/adorabyssal-oracle)** in Chrome
2. Tap the **📲 INSTALL APP** button at the bottom of the screen
3. Confirm — the app will appear on your home screen

## Installing on iOS

1. Open the link in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** icon ⎋
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add**
